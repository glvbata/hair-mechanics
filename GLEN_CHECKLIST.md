## Getting More Reviews (Ongoing)

**Goal: Get to 100+ reviews with recent dates (within 90 days)**

### The Easy Way — QR Code Card
Print a small card to hand to every client after their cut:

> *"Loved your cut? Leave us a review — it only takes 30 seconds."*
> [QR code linking to your Google review page]

To get your review link:
1. Go to business.google.com
2. Click "Get more reviews"
3. Copy the short link — use a QR code generator to turn it into a QR code

### What to Say (verbal)
After finishing a cut:  
*"If you liked it, I'd really appreciate a Google review — it helps a lot. I can text you the link if you want."*

### Things That Help
- [ ] Reply to every review (5 stars and bad ones) — Google rewards engagement
- [ ] Ask right after the cut, not days later
- [ ] Text the review link to regulars who say they'll do it

## Weekly Specials (Post Every Week)

Go to: **business.google.com → Posts → Add update**

Post once a week, every week. Google boosts listings that stay active.

### What to Post

**Monday or Tuesday** — post the week's special:

> **"Walk-In Special — [Month] [Week]"**
> This week: Kids Cuts $35 + free line-up. Open Mon-Fri 10am-8pm, Sat-Sun 8am-8pm. Walk-ins always welcome. Call (206) 399-9288.

Rotate these week to week:
- "Walk-ins welcome all week — no appointment needed"
- "Kids back in school? Kids cuts $35, any day this week"
- "Look fresh for the weekend — come in Friday or Saturday"
- "Beard + haircut combo $50 — full groom, 7 days a week"
- "Open late until 8pm — come in after work, no appointment needed"

**Tips:**
- Add a photo every time (use a haircut photo from your phone)
- Keep it short — 2-3 sentences max
- Always end with the phone number or hours
